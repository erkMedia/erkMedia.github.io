erk Media Ltd is a research, innovation, and design hub.

Our core ideology is to promote interdisciplinary, evidence-based research, and creative, playful design content. We draw on expertise in emerging technology, network theory, ethics, policy, and the creative arts.

Our experts are selected due to their explicit desire to create positive change.

We curate advancement throughout the technological, societal, and political spectrum; driven by curiousity, creativity, honesty, transparency, and accountability.
